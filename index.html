The Hats I Wear

UX Engineer

I use my developer background and design knowledge to bridge the gap between developers and designers. I enjoy being able to design and create products that are user-friendly and easy for developers to build.

SCSS Architect

I can make CSS/SCSS bend to my will and have created Arrow, a framework and architecture based on that. Arrow allows developers to apply styles without worrying about visual design or CSS pitfalls like overrides, while utilizing reusable, maintainable and scalable code.

Product Designer

My process starts with what the user's goals are and takes into account business requirements, UX principles and technical limitations to create a clean and enjoyable experience. No detail is too small, whether I'm creating user flows, mockups or designing the final product.

Graphic Designer

Even a great product won't attract customers if it's not well represented. It doesn't matter if it's a website or branding materials, I enjoy creating beautiful visual designs with precision and attention to detail that will withstand the test of time.
